About the Project

This project analyzes Electric Vehicle charging infrastructure and vehicle range data across India. The data is stored in MySQL, visualized in Tableau, and presented through a Flask web application. Users can explore an interactive Dashboard and Story to understand charging stations, vehicle manufacturers, battery range, charging capacity and overall EV trends.
